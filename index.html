Electric Vehicles

Electric Vehicles and Plug-in Hybrid Vehicles are becoming more and more noticeable on Irish roads over the last number of years, but, the number of EVs and PHEVs are going to increase even further in the coming years. This is down to many reasons, but the biggest factor is probably that from 2030, consumers will not be able to buy new petrol or diesel cars. This is part of Irelands aim to reduce its carbon footprint. There are similar measures to be introduced in the UK.
One issue people currently have with Electric Vehicles is charging them. Charging points are very limited in this country, and therefore an EV driver must know where to find them. That is the main purpose of this website. Scroll through the map above to locate your nearest charging point wherever you are in the country.

Browse through the site to learn a little more about some of the Electric cars currently available to buy in Ireland. If you have any questions about any of these cars or any other EVs that are not on the list, just contact us using the form on out contact page.
Heres to moving forward to a cleaner Ireland for all.
