Volkswagen ID3

The Volkswagen ID 3 is Volkswagens first purpose built Electric Vehicle. Unlike the e-Golf, which was based on Volkswagens bestselling hatchback, the ID3 was designed from the ground up to be an electric vehicle. It is the first in a range of Electric Vehicles made by Volkswagen including the very impressive ID4 which was unveiled earlier this year.

    Price: From € 32,650
    Battery size: 58kwh, 77kwh
    Range: 430 - 580km
    Power (HP): 204
    Acceleration 0-100kph: 7.3

Tesla Model 3

Tesla’s Model 3 is the younger sibling of the brands flagship Model S. It was created to rival the mid-sized executive saloon vehicles such as the BMW 3 Series, Audi A4 and Mercedes Class. Although smaller than the Model S, The Model 3 does not lack style or performance.

    Price: From € 48,900
    Battery size: 70kwh
    Range: 418km – 542km
    Power (HP): 580
    Acceleration 0-100kph: 3.3

Nissan Leaf

The Nissan Leaf is Irelands most popular Electric Vehicle. First introduced in 2015, the Leaf is now on its second iteration. The Leaf originally began with a 24kwh battery, but now boasts up to a 62kwh battery. It also comes with a host of new technologies such as Nissans e-pedal, and Pro-Pilot.

    Price: From € 39,890
    Battery size: 40kwh, 62kwh
    Range: 385km
    Power (HP): 150 - 217
    Acceleration 0-100kph: 6.5

Hyundai Kona

The Kona is Hyundai’s second EV after the Ioniq. Sitting in the increasingly popular small SUV category, the Kona will offer a real eco-friendly alternative to best selling cars such as the VW T-Roc, Ford Kuga, Peugeot 2008 and Seat Arona. It will however meet fierce competition from Fords latest EV, the reinvented Puma.

    Price: From € 39,300
    Battery size: 64kwh
    Range: 449km
    Power (KW): 150
    Acceleration 0-100kph: 7.6

BMW 330e

BMW’s 330e is the only PHEV on this list. Based on the ever reliable 3 Series the 330e has the perfect mix of luxury and driving pleasure, and, it is now cleaner and more economical than ever before. A 2.0l petrol engine combined with an electric motor, the 330e could be the ideal alternative for those who aren’t ready to go for a full electric just yet.

    Price: From € 53,104
    Electric Range: 71km
    Power (BHP): 292
    Acceleration 0-100kph: 5.9
